We Are Quality Laundry Provider In Your City

Eli’s Laundry was founded by Tawiah Elijah, a dedicated young man with a passion for service and community development. As the head of Eli’s Foundation, a philanthropic initiative aimed at uplifting lives, his commitment to helping others has always been at the heart of his journey. His inspiration to start a laundry business stemmed from witnessing how tedious and time-consuming laundry could be for many individuals and families. With a deep desire to provide convenience and quality, he began humbly—offering simple laundry services with care and precision. Through hard work, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Eli’s Laundry has grown into a distinguished and trusted laundry provider in Doryuma, Yedidia Base.
